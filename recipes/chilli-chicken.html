Chilli Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken: You can use boneless chicken breast or thigh, cut into bite-sized pieces. You will need about 500 grams of chicken for this recipe.
  • Buttermilk: This is used to marinate the chicken and make it tender and juicy. You will need about 1 cup of buttermilk.
  • Cornflour and plain flour: These are used to coat the chicken before frying. You will need about 3 tablespoons of cornflour and 2 tablespoons of plain flour.
  • Spices: You will need garlic powder, chilli powder, salt and pepper for seasoning the chicken. You will also need red chilli powder, garam masala, and dried mint for the sauce.
  • Egg: This is used to bind the flour coating and make it crispy. You will need one egg, lightly beaten.
  • Soy sauce and chilli sauce: These are used to marinate the chicken and add flavor to the sauce. You will need about 2 tablespoons of each.
  • Ketchup: This is used to add sweetness and tanginess to the sauce. You will need about 1/4 cup of ketchup.
  • Vinegar: This is used to add acidity and balance the flavors of the sauce. You will need about 1 tablespoon of vinegar.
  • Sugar: This is used to enhance the sweetness of the sauce. You will need about 1 teaspoon of sugar.
  • Oil: This is used to fry the chicken and sautÃ© the vegetables. You will need about 1/4 cup of oil.
  • Garlic: This is used to add aroma and flavor to the dish. You will need about 6 cloves of garlic, finely chopped.
  • Onion: This is used to add texture and sweetness to the dish. You will need one medium onion, sliced.
  • Capsicum: This is used to add crunch and color to the dish. You can use green, red, or yellow capsicum, or a mix of them. You will need one large capsicum, diced.
  • Green chillies: These are used to add heat and spice to the dish. You can adjust the number of green chillies according to your taste preference. You will need about 2 to 4 green chillies, slit.
  • Spring onion: This is used to garnish the dish and add freshness. You will need about 2 tablespoons of spring onion greens, chopped.

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, add boneless chicken pieces, salt, cornflour, black pepper, egg, soy sauce and red chilli sauce. Mix well and keep it for marination for about 20 minutes.
  2. Fry the chicken: Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the chicken pieces until golden and crisp. Drain them on a paper towel and keep aside .
  3. Prepare the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, red chilli powder, red chilli sauce and ketchup. This will be the sauce for seasoning the chicken.
  4. Saute the vegetables: In another pan, heat some oil and fry chopped garlic until golden. Then add sliced onion, diced capsicum and slit green chillies. Saute them for a few minutes until they are slightly soft .
  5. Add the sauce and chicken: Pour the prepared sauce over the vegetables and bring it to a boil. Then add the fried chicken pieces and toss well to coat them with the sauce. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken well .
